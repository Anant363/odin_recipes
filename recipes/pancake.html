Pancake Recipe

Description

These classic homemade pancakes are light, fluffy, and super easy to make. Perfect for a weekend breakfast with syrup or toppings of your choice.

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients just until blended.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, flip and cook the other side.
