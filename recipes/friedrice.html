Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This easy and flavorful fried rice is a great way to use leftover rice and customize with your favorite veggies and proteins.

  • Cooked Rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs in a wok or skillet and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables until tender.
  3. Add rice and mix well with the veggies.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil.
  5. Add scrambled eggs back and mix thoroughly.
